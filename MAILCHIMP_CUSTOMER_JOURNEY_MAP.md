## Missing Email Automations

**Current state:** 0 automations configured

**Major Missed Opportunities:**

### 1. Welcome Series (New Subscriber)
**Trigger:** Someone subscribes to email list
**Recommended sequence:**
- Day 0: Welcome email with facility overview, hours, what to expect
- Day 2: Intro to membership options with comparison chart
- Day 5: Popular classes and how to book
- Day 7: Community events calendar and social media links

### 2. New Member Onboarding (Post-Purchase)
**Trigger:** First membership purchase in Capitan
**Recommended sequence:**
- Day 0: Congratulations! Here's what's included, how to check in, parking info
- Day 3: Don't forget - free intro class for new members, book now
- Day 7: Meet the community - upcoming social events
- Day 14: How are you doing? Feedback request + personal training offer

### 3. Class Registration Follow-up
**Trigger:** Someone registers for intro/lead class
**Recommended sequence:**
- 2 days before: Reminder + what to bring + arrival time
- 1 day after: How was the class? Next steps in your climbing journey
- 1 week after: Ready for the next level? [Next class recommendation]

### 4. Event Registration Follow-up
**Trigger:** Registers for comp, birthday party, or special event
**Recommended sequence:**
- 1 week before: Event details reminder + what to bring
- 1 day before: See you tomorrow! Parking, schedule, contact info
- 1 day after: Thanks for coming! Share photos, tag us, next event preview

### 5. Lapsed Member Re-engagement
**Trigger:** Membership expired 30+ days ago
**Recommended sequence:**
- Day 30: We miss you! Here's what's new since you've been gone
- Day 45: Special win-back offer - 15% off membership renewal
- Day 60: Last chance - offer expires soon + testimonials from active members

### 6. Engagement-Based Nurture
**Trigger:** Opens 3+ emails but never clicks
**Recommended sequence:**
- Send personalized email: "We noticed you're interested but haven't visited - can we help you get started?"
- Include: FAQ, first-timer guide, special first-visit discount

## Key Recommendations

### Immediate Fixes (Quick Wins)

1. **Add CTAs to Informational Emails**
   - "This Week at Basin" newsletter needs booking links for every event mentioned
   - Even facility updates should include "Book a Visit" or "View Schedule" CTA

2. **Standardize CTA Strategy**
   - Every email should have 1-2 clear, action-oriented CTAs
   - Link directly to Capitan booking pages, not Mailchimp landing pages
   - Use button format, not just text links

3. **Set Up Basic Automations (Start with 3)**
   - Welcome series for new subscribers
   - New member onboarding
   - Event registration reminders

### Medium-Term Improvements (30-60 days)

4. **Implement Segmentation**
   - Start with Active Members vs Non-Members
   - Different messaging drives better conversion

5. **Create Email Template Library**
   - Event promotion template (clear CTA format)
   - Class announcement template
   - Member update template
   - All should have consistent CTA placement and design

6. **A/B Test Subject Lines**
   - Test emoji vs no emoji
   - Test urgency ("Last Chance" vs "Don't Miss")
   - Test specificity ("Boulder Bash This Saturday" vs "Upcoming Competition")

### Long-Term Strategy (90+ days)

7. **Behavioral Automation Flows**
   - Cart abandonment (started class registration but didn't complete)
   - Post-visit follow-up (checked in via Capitan)
   - Milestone celebrations (50 check-ins, 6-month member anniversary)

8. **Content Calendar Integration**
   - Sync with Instagram posting schedule
   - Coordinate email themes with in-gym promotions
   - Plan seasonal campaigns in advance

9. **Advanced Segmentation**
   - Climbing level (beginner vs advanced)
   - Interest-based (fitness vs climbing vs kids programs)
   - Engagement tier (super fans vs casual)
   - Geographic (if expanding locations)
