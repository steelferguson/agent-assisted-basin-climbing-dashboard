# Privacy Policy
**Effective date:** November 8, 2025

## 1. Overview
This application ("the App") is operated by **Basin Climbing and Fitness** ("Basin," "we," "our").
The App connects securely to QuickBooks Online to access financial data for Basin's internal business reporting and analysis.

## 2. Information We Collect
The App collects data from Basin's QuickBooks Online account, including but not limited to:
- Transaction details (sales, invoices, payments, refunds)
- Account balances and reports
- Related metadata required to reconcile financial information

No customer-identifiable information beyond what exists in Basin's accounting records is intentionally stored or shared.

## 3. Purpose of Collection
Collected information is used **solely for internal Basin leadership and management reporting** to monitor business performance and maintain accurate financial oversight.

## 4. Data Storage & Retention
Data is stored in secure, access-controlled environments. Information is retained only as long as necessary for accounting and reporting purposes.

## 5. Sharing & Disclosure
Data accessed through QuickBooks is **not shared** with third parties, sold, or disclosed outside Basin Climbing and Fitness except as required by law.

## 6. Security
The App is password-protected and access is limited to authorized Basin team members.
Industry-standard encryption and authentication mechanisms are used to protect data in transit and at rest.
